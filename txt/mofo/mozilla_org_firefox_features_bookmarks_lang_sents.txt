File that bookmark
Get your faves all sorted with the bookmark star icon, which lets you add custom names and folders quickly. Then dial in your bookmarks toolbar to make sure you never lose sight of the links you love.
Fly with that bookmark
Take your favorites on the fly. Use Firefox Sync to access your bookmarks across all your devices, from desktop to mobile. Or try Pocket to save any online article, or page and come back to later – even without internet.
